About me


I am ACATO Amon, a GIS/Remote Sensing scientist, Surveyor and Data Science enthusiast who is passionate about data and innovative ways that data can improve and influence decision making with a specific bias on spatial data.

Along my professional journey, I have honed various skills at differernt levels of spatial data management ranging from data collection, data cleaning and storage, data analysis and information dissemination. My first degrees in Surveying and Geomatics gives me a strong background in data collection methodologies for both primary and secondary data capture. I later on developed interest in cleaning and analysing the collected data to support evidance based decision making. It is against this background that I sought GIS knowledge through both formal education (MSc. Geo-Information Science and Technology) and self taught skills like Python programming and data visualisation skills. In my masters thesis titled "Estimation of ambient outdoor air quality in data poor locations using open access data, a case study of Kampala", I applied GIS, Remote Sensing and programming skills to develop a Random Forest model for estimating a spatially continuous surface of PM2.5.

Some of my recent projects that demonstrate my skills are shared under "Projects". I can be contacted at all times through the contact information at the bottom of the page

KNOWLEDGE AREAS


  • Data collection

    Owing to my background in Surveying, I am knowledgeable in a wide range of mobile mapping and data collection tools.
    I am proficient in the design and customisation of data collection forms for mobile data collection.

  • Data cleaning and analysis

    I deploy tools like Microsoft Excel, Python, ArcGIS and Q-GIS to both clean and analyse the collected data into actionable information.

  • Information dissemination

    With tools like ArcGIS Enterprise, Microsoft PowerBI and Tableau along with my web mappping skills, I am able to create and share various cartographic both as paper documents and on the web.

  • Remote Sensing

    Using tools like ArcGIS, Q-GIS, Erdas Imagine and Python, I am able to process and analyse remotely sensed imagery to derive useful insights like urban heat Islands, environmental pollution levels and endangered natural resourcces among others. I also know the different sources of remotely sensed data.

  • Programming

    I can use Python to clean and analyse differnt types of data. I can also use the combination of HTML, CSS, Javascript and supporting tools like GeoServer and Leaflet to share cartographic products on the web.
